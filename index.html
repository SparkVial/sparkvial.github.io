What is SparkVial

A project to create a series of sensor and actuator devices that connect via USB/UART/LVDS to either a smartphone, a PC or a dedicated logging device, along with great software to interface with them.

The main goal is to inspire students to persue science and technology fields by providing intuitive and interactive visualizations of the world around them.

Goals

  • Provide robust, high-quality, and affordable hardware which is OSHW.
  • Provide user-friendly, high-quality, cross-platform software to interface with them which is FOSS.
  • Provide pre-made teaching materials and experiment booklets (for free).
