CatEars

The CatEars turn any steady supply of 3.3v and a UART line into a working SparkVial board.

Naming convention

The CatEars have the following naming convention:

CatEars-{4,8,16}x{B,A,R}-{E,R}{N,W,R}-{1}

  • I/O
    • Number of outputs
    • Connector type
      • Bare, simply 4 pins that can be soldered to.
      • Aux / 3.5mm 4 pole female jack
      • RJ11 6P4C
  • Quality & Reliability
    • Part sourcing
      • Economy (Aliexpress, Alibaba, etc.)
      • Robust (Digikey, Mouser, etc.)
    • Board enclosure
      • No enclosure, just a PCB
      • Water-resistant, Covered in a protective layer, has wires for I/O
      • Robust, Covered in potting compound, has wires for I/O
  • Revision
